Power BI 建模與 DAX 核心公式參考

1. 基礎度量值 (Base)

Total_Expense = SUM(Expense[Actual_Amount])
Budget_Expense = SUM(Expense[Budget_Amount])

用途：所有進階計算的基礎模組。

2. 差異與達成率 (Variance)

Variance = [Total_Expense] - [Budget_Expense]
Achieve_Rate = DIVIDE([Total_Expense], [Budget_Expense], 0)

用途：衡量實際與目標的差距，識別超支狀態。

3. 時間智慧 (Time Intelligence)

Expense_YTD = TOTALYTD([Total_Expense], 'Date'[Date])
Expense_LastYear = CALCULATE([Total_Expense], SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR('Date'[Date]))
YoY_Pct = DIVIDE([Total_Expense] - [Expense_LastYear], [Expense_LastYear], 0)

用途：計算年初至今累計(YTD)與年度同比(YoY)。

4. 排名與占比 (Ranking & Pct)

Pct_of_Total = 
DIVIDE([Total_Expense], CALCULATE([Total_Expense], ALL(Expense_Category)), 0)

Rank_by_Dept = RANKX(ALL(Department), [Total_Expense], , DESC)

用途：找出消耗資源最大的部門或專案。
